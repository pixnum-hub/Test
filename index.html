How To Use

📡 Auto Compass

Tap Enable Compass and grant sensor permission. Hold phone flat. On iOS move in a figure-8 to calibrate.

✋ Manual Mode

  • Drag dial to rotate freely
  • ±1°/10°/45° nudge buttons
  • Slider sweeps full 360°
  • Reset button → 0° North

🔒 Lock Bearing

Freezes needle at current heading. Red pulsing ring appears. Tap Unlock to resume.

💾 Save & Share

Save any bearing with a label (persists across sessions). Share via native share sheet or clipboard copy.

🧲 True North

Switch to True North mode, then dial in or auto-detect your local declination with 📍.

🛠 15 Vastu Tools

Scroll down and tap any tool tab: Sitting direction, Sleep guide, Room mapper, Bearing calculator, Zone boundaries, Sub-directions, Remedies, Door analyzer, Planets, Birth direction, Muhurta, Checklist, Compass Rose, GPS tracker, Colour guide.

⌨ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Arrows — nudge ±1° · Shift+Arrows — ±10°
  • L lock · S save · N north · M mode · T north type · G glossary · Esc close

📲 Install as PWA

Android: browser menu → Add to Home Screen. iOS Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen. Works offline.
