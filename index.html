Navāṃśa Cakra — D9 Chart (Dharma & Marriage)

The Navāṃśa chart divides each sign into 9 parts of 3°20′ each, revealing the soul's purpose, marriage partner qualities, and the inner chart underlying the birth chart.

Hora Chart — D2 (Wealth)

Divides each sign into two 15° halves. Odd signs: 1st half = Sun hora (Leo), 2nd half = Moon hora (Cancer). Even signs reversed. Reveals financial karma.

Ṣaḍbala — Six-fold Planetary Strength

Ṣaḍbala measures planetary strength through six sources: positional (Sthāna), directional (Dig), temporal (Kāla), motion (Chesta), natural (Naisargika), and aspectual (Dṛk) strengths.

Aṣṭakavarga — Eight-Source Point System

Each of the 7 planets and Lagna contribute points to each sign (0–8). Scores of 5+ are favorable; 4 is neutral; 3 or below indicate challenges when planets transit that sign.
